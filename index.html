About

I am currently a Research Scientist working on 3D generative AI with diffusion models. Meanwhile, I am also advised by [Prof. Qiang Liu](https://www.cs.utexas.edu/~lqiang/) for my graduate degree.

Previously, I spent five years at SambaNova Systems as a senior tech lead working on software and hardware codesign.

Before that, I obtained my B.S. in CS from UIUC advised by Prof. Bo Li.
